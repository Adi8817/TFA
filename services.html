Commercial Property

We specialise in sourcing, leasing, and selling high-level commercial properties across Australia, with a focus on delivering strategic investment outcomes tailored to the needs of our clients. Our portfolio includes a diverse range of assets which include office spaces, retail environments, industrial facilities, and mixed use developments carefully selected to align with investor goals and market demand.

Business Investments

CBD Business broking service offer personalised business broking journey designed to support investors and entrepreneurs through every stage of buying, selling, or valuing a commercial business. With deep expertise across a wide range of industries — including retail, hospitality, healthcare, logistics, and professional services — we tailor our approach to meet the specific needs and objectives of each client.

Our services begin with a thorough understanding of your investment goals or business exit plans. For buyers, we source high-quality businesses with solid financial performance, operational systems in place, and clear growth potential—often including off-market opportunities that are not publicly listed. For sellers, we provide accurate business valuations, detailed preparation of documentation, and targeted marketing strategies to attract qualified buyers and achieve the best possible sale outcome.

Development Sites

With a keen eye for high-growth corridors and strategic landholdings, at CBD we specialise in identifying and delivering premium development site opportunities that offer both long-term profitability and transformational potential. We work closely with investors, developers, and landowners to secure sites in locations poised for future infrastructure investment, population growth, and urban renewal.

Our in-depth market research, combined with local area insight, allows us to pinpoint parcels of land that are not only suitable for residential, commercial, or mixed-use development, but are also aligned with zoning advantages, planning approvals, and local government development agendas. Whether it’s infill redevelopment in metropolitan hubs or greenfield sites in emerging regional centres, we focus on opportunities with strong fundamentals and sustainable value creation.
