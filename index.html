We are
Total Football Academie

Total Football Academie is a future-focused football training institution dedicated to nurturing young talent from the grassroots level.

Driven by a passion for building strong, skilled, and intelligent footballers, we deliver value through structured coaching programs and a holistic development model.

At TFA, we offer more than just training — we build confidence, discipline, and pathways to success, both on and off the pitch.

What Sets Us Apart

Game Intelligence, Not Just Training: At Total Football Academie, we go beyond drills — we shape thinking footballers. Our programs build players who read the game, adapt under pressure, and make smarter decisions on the pitch.

We take a personalised approach, understanding each player's journey and tailoring coaching to unlock their full potential — whether they’re beginners or elite prospects.

Our experienced FFA-licensed coaches bring national and international insights, ensuring players are ready for the modern game — technically, tactically, and mentally.

TFA - Head Coach / Technical Director SAT SANGHERA

Sat Sanghera is a passionate and experienced football coach with a strong track record in youth development and high-performance environments. He has coached the AFA Australian Youth Futsal Team, supporting thegrowth of emerging talent on both national and international stages.

With a lifelong connection to the game, Sat blends technical knowledge, tactical insight, and strong communication to bring the best out of his players. His coaching philosophy focuses on building confident, intelligent footballers through discipline, teamwork, and a deep understanding of the game.

Values and Vision

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in everything we do – from training and education to behavior and performance – striving for continuous improvement on and off the pitch.

Integrity

We act with honesty, transparency, and respect. We build trust by doing the right thing, even when no one is watching.

Development

We prioritize long-term player growth over short-term results, nurturing technical, physical, tactical, and personal development through individualized support and mentorship.

Resilience

We foster mental toughness, adaptability, and the courage to overcome challenges – qualities essential for success in football and life.

Unity

We cultivate a strong team culture where diversity is embraced, roles are valued, and collective success is celebrated above individual accolades.
