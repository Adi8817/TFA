Football Kick-Off (Ages 5–6)

Football Kick-Off (Ages 5–6) Introduction to the World Game Our Football Initiation Program is designed to help young children explore movement, coordination, and teamwork through fun, ball-focused activities. Why Choose Our Program? Experienced Coaching – Led by Sat, a specialist in the Discovery and Skill Acquisition phases, with over 15 years of coaching experience. Quality Facilities – Training takes place on a safe, indoor futsal court keeping play continuous and focused. Small Group Focus – Low player-to-coach ratios ensure personalised attention and effective learning. Engaging Sessions – Kids are always active with the ball, learning through play, not waiting in lines.

Skills Development (Ages 10–11)

Sharpening Football Intelligence Often called the “golden age of motor learning,” this stage is marked by high motivation, growing commitment, and a desire to perform well. Players are eager to improve, work hard in training, and demonstrate their skills in games. Building on the fundamentals from the Football Basics Program, players at this level are ready to handle more complex game situations and decision-making. Football is about reading the game and reacting effectively. We focus on developing game intelligence through three key scenarios: Loose-Ball Games – Competing for possession when the ball is free; winning loose balls often decides the outcome. Attacking Games – Making smart decisions while in possession and creating scoring opportunities.

Game Development (Ages 12–14+)

Mastering the Full Game At this stage, players develop a lasting passion for football through engaging, well-structured programs that match their interests and goals. Building on the Football Skills Program, players are now ready for more advanced, tactical play. They respond well to teamwork and collaborative training, making this an ideal age to strengthen team concepts. Program Focus: Intelligence In Attack • The positional attack (building up from the back): its start and progression • Keeping possession of the ball • Attacking from the midfield

Professional Programs & Development

1. Professional Programs

Elite Player Development Program (U13-U18)

Objective:

Prepare players for professional or collegiate pathways.

Structure:

  • 4-5 training sessions/week + weekend fixtures
  • Periodized seasonal plan (Pre-season, In-season, Off-season)
  • Weekly tactical reviews & video analysis
  • Strength & Conditioning support (age-appropriate)
  • Individual Development Plans (IDPs)
  • Access to professional scouts & trial opportunities

Pre-Academy Program (U8-U12)

Objective:

Build foundational technical and decision-making skills.

Focus Areas:

  • Ball mastery, 1v1 dominance, movement, and spatial awareness
  • Introduction to game principles
  • Fun, engaging, high-tempo sessions

Professional Pathway Residency (Invite Only)

Full-time residential program for top-tier prospects. Includes scheduling, daily training, nutrition, recovery, and mentorship.

Direct pipeline to professional clubs & international opportunities.

2. Development Initiatives

Coach the Coaches Program

Professional development for grassroots and academy-level coaches.

Workshops led by FIFA/AFC licensed instructors.

Promotes consistency in player development philosophy.

Talent ID & Community Outreach

Regular Talent ID camps in regional and underserved communities.

Partnership with schools and grassroots clubs.

Scholarships offered to high-potential players.

Academic & Life Skills Integration

Education programs ensure academy players ensure balanced development.

Life skills workshops: leadership, communication, media training.

Parent Education Series

Equips families with knowledge on football pathways, nutrition, and emotional support frameworks.

3. Player Development Philosophy

Technical Excellence

Emphasis on first touch, passing range, receiving under pressure.

Intensive focus on weak foot development and positional techniques.

Tactical Intelligence

Understanding of multiple formations and systems of play.

Position-specific training.

Video-based game analysis and tactical education.

Physical Conditioning

Age-specific athletic development with performance testing.

Injury prevention, mobility, and recovery protocols embedded.

Mental Toughness & Character Building

Sports psychology sessions: mindset, focus, and resilience.

Leadership roles and team-building challenges.

  • Showcase tournaments & global academy partnerships
